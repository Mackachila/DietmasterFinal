About DietMaster

We're on a mission to improve child nutrition and fight malnutrition in Kenya's coastal region through innovative technology.

Our Story

Founded in 2025 by Jared Mackachila in Kilifi County, DietMaster was born from a desire to address childhood malnutrition in Kenya's coastal region. After seeing children under 5 struggling with nutritional deficiencies, we created an AI-powered solution that considers local food availability, cultural preferences, and nutritional science.

Our Approach

We combine cutting-edge technology with nutritional expertise to create personalized meal plans. Our system analyzes a child's specific needs, local food availability, and cultural preferences to recommend balanced, nutritious meals that support optimal development during these crucial early years.

Our Impact

Since our launch, we've helped over 5,000 families in Kilifi County improve their children's nutrition. Our partnerships with local healthcare providers and community organizations have allowed us to reach even the most remote areas, making proper nutrition accessible to all.

Our Mission

To combat childhood malnutrition in Kenya through innovative, accessible, and culturally-sensitive nutrition technology that empowers parents to make informed food choices for their children.

We believe every child deserves access to proper nutrition during their formative years, regardless of socioeconomic status or geographic location. Through our technology, we aim to make nutritional guidance accessible to all families with young children.

Our Vision

To create a future where childhood malnutrition is eliminated in Kenya and throughout Africa, with technology serving as a bridge between nutritional science and everyday practical implementation.

We envision communities where all children under 5 years receive optimal nutrition for their development, leading to healthier populations, stronger communities, and brighter futures for Kenya's next generation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our service and how DietMaster can help your child

How does DietMaster create personalized meal recommendations?

DietMaster uses a combination of artificial intelligence and expert nutritional guidelines to analyze your child's age, weight, dietary restrictions, and preferences. We also consider local food availability in Kilifi County to ensure our recommendations are practical and accessible. Our system then generates balanced meal plans that meet all nutritional requirements for optimal development.

Is DietMaster suitable for children with allergies or special dietary needs?

Absolutely! During the initial assessment, we collect detailed information about your child's allergies, intolerances, and any special dietary needs. Our algorithm then carefully excludes problematic foods while ensuring nutritional needs are still met. We also provide alternative suggestions for common allergens found in traditional Kenyan coastal cuisine.

How often are meal plans updated for growing children?

Children's nutritional needs change rapidly during the first five years of life. We recommend updating your child's profile every three months for children under 2, and every six months for children 2-5 years old. The system will automatically adjust recommendations based on these updates, accounting for developmental stages, growth, and changing nutritional requirements.

Does DietMaster take into account local Kenyan coastal foods?

Yes, this is one of our key strengths! Our database includes a comprehensive collection of foods commonly available in Kilifi County and the wider coastal region. We emphasize local fruits, vegetables, grains, and protein sources that are nutritionally rich and culturally appropriate. Our recommendations incorporate traditional coastal Kenyan ingredients like cassava, coconut, fish, mangoes, and indigenous vegetables.

How can I access DietMaster if I don't have regular internet access?

We understand that internet connectivity can be limited in some parts of Kilifi County. That's why we've developed an SMS-based system for families without regular internet access. You can receive simplified weekly meal plans via text message, and our mobile teams regularly visit communities to provide in-person support and printed materials. We also partner with local health centers where you can access our system.
